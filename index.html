Instructions

1. Type any letter to start playing the game.

2. You will have 10 guesses to choose the correct letter that the computer is guessing.

3. Your guesses, guesses left, wins, and losses will appear on the left.

4. If you don't get the correct letter after 10 guesses, an alert will pop up saying that you lost.

5. When you guess the correct letter, an alert will pop up saying that you have won and prints the winning letter.
